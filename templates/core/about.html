About EarnWave

We built EarnWave to make the internet pay everyday Nigerians back for their time and attention.

Our Mission

In Nigeria, data and airtime are essential. Yet brands spend billions getting attention without paying the people who give it. EarnWave bridges that gap — giving users real, tangible rewards (airtime) for their time, opinions, and knowledge.

We believe every Nigerian deserves to benefit from the attention economy. Every survey you complete funds a brand's research. Every ad you watch pays an advertiser's bill. EarnWave makes sure you get your cut.
